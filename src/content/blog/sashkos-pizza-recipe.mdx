# Sashko's Homemade Pizza Recipe

There's nothing quite like the smell of a freshly baked homemade pizza coming out of the oven. This is my go-to pizza recipe that I've perfected over time. It's simple, delicious, and the dough is incredibly easy to make!

## Ingredients

### For the Dough
- 500g all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp sugar
- Half a packet of instant yeast (I use "saf-instant", but any instant yeast works)
- 300ml warm water
- 4 tbsp olive oil

### For the Sauce
- 150-200g tomato paste
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Italian herbs (oregano, basil, or Italian seasoning blend)
- Hot sauce or chili flakes (optional)

### Toppings
- Pepperoni (or any similar salami)
- Mozzarella cheese
- Aged cheese like Maasdam, Radamer, or any flavorful hard cheese
- Mushrooms (optional)
- Olives (optional)

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
  The secret to a great pizza is using two types of cheese — mozzarella for that classic stretch and an aged cheese for extra flavor!
</Callout>

## Making the Dough

1. **Sift the flour** — Sift 500g of flour into a large bowl and make a well in the center.

2. **Add ingredients**:
   - 1 tsp salt
   - 2 tsp sugar
   - 11g of instant yeast (Half a packet of "saf-instant")
   - 300ml warm water
   - 4 tbsp olive oil

3. **Knead the dough** — Mix everything together until you get a smooth, elastic dough. Don't overwork it!

4. **Let it rise** — Cover the bowl with a kitchen towel and leave it in a warm place for about 1 hour until it doubles in size.

<Callout type="warning" title="Temperature Matters">
  The water should be warm (around 40°C / 104°F), not hot. Hot water will kill the yeast!
</Callout>

## Making the Pizza

**Preheat your oven** to 200°C (392°F). If you have a modern oven with convection, use the fan-assisted mode with top and bottom heating elements.

### Step 1: Prepare the Dough
Punch down the risen dough and knead it briefly. Roll it out to fit your baking pan.

### Step 2: Shape the Pizza
Place the dough on your baking pan and shape the edges to form a crust. Brush the crust with olive oil for a golden finish.

### Step 3: Make the Sauce
Mix together:
- 150-200g tomato paste
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- A generous amount of Italian herbs
- Hot sauce or chili flakes (if you like it spicy)

<Callout type="tip" title="Quick Alternative">
  Don't have time to make sauce? You can use store-bought pizza sauce or pasta sauce instead. Just spread it on and you're good to go!
</Callout>

### Step 4: Assemble
1. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, leaving the crust clean.
2. Arrange pepperoni slices on top.
3. Add mushrooms and olives if using.

### Step 5: First Bake
Put the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for **15 minutes**.

### Step 6: Add the Cheese
At the 15-minute mark, take the pizza out quickly and add:
- Mozzarella (torn into pieces or sliced)
- Grated aged cheese on top

### Step 7: Final Bake
Return the pizza to the oven for another **5 minutes** until the cheese is melted and bubbly with golden spots.

<Callout type="tip" title="Why Add Cheese Later?">
  Adding cheese in the last 5 minutes prevents it from burning and ensures you get that perfect melty, stretchy texture!
</Callout>

## Done! 🍕

Take it out, let it cool for a minute (if you can resist), slice it up, and enjoy your homemade masterpiece!

## Quick Reference

**Total active time:** ~30 minutes  
**Total time including rising:** ~1.5 hours  
**Serves:** 4-6 people (one large pizza)
